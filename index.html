A HOOD-PASS is the official on-chain access credential of THE GOODLUMS — the world's first post-apocalyptic internet gang. It proves your membership status and grants access to exclusive benefits, airdrops, and recognition within the community.

⚠️ ONLY OFFICIAL MEMBERS OF THE GOODLUMS CAN OBTAIN A VALID/VALIDATED HOOD-PASS ⚠️
